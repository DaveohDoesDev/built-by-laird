About

Built by Laird is an independent LLC and a studio of one. Every project is hand-built — no themes, no Wix, no template marketplaces. I work with three to four clients a year, on purpose, so each site gets the attention it deserves.

I care about three things, in this order: that your customers find you, that the site looks like it belongs to your business and no one else's, and that you own everything when we're done — the code, the domain, the hosting setup, the keys.
