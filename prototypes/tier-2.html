About
Portrait — placeholder

I'm David Laird. I run a small studio that designs and builds websites for businesses around here. The kind of sites that show up when someone Googles you, load fast, look like they belong to you, and don't fall apart in two years.

Built by Laird is an independent LLC. Every project is hand-built — no themes, no Wix, no template marketplaces. I work with three to four clients a year, on purpose. If you'd like to talk about a project, the calendar opens with an email.

How it works · 4 phases
01 · 30 min
Discover
A short conversation. What does your business actually need this site to do? Who's it for? We sketch the structure together.
02 · 1 wk
Plan
A written proposal — pages, scope, price, timeline. No surprises later. You sign or you don't, no pressure.
03 · 3 — 6 wk
Build
I build, you review at two checkpoints. We refine. Real code, mobile-first, fast on weak phones, accessible.
04 · 1 day
Launch
We ship. You own the code, the domain, the hosting. I show you how to update copy. Optional ongoing support.
